Personal Introduction

my journey to game development started in Polytechnic but my passion for games sparked much sooner. It started as a simple interest in playing games, before having the urge to make my own game and thus pursuing a course in Game development which landed me here in Nanyang Polytechnic. I enjoy making fast-paced action games and am a massive fan of the mecha genre when it comes to games. I enjoy working in teams as i believe a tightly knit community working on a shared goal is great for forming bonds. Working in a team also allows me to broaden my horizons, view different perspectives, and overall improve as a developer.
