About Me

Hi there, I'm Loo Ezen and I am currently studying Game Developent. Games themselves have always been a big part of my life as well as my upbringing. I have played games from all types of genres and discovered game development in the later parts of my life, but I never looked back once I did.


Though inexperienced compared to many others, my love for playing and making games has only grown since I started, and I will continue to pursue this passion for as long as I am able.


Outside of Games, I enjoy writing poetry, practicing Taekwondo, and building model kits in my free time. Though I tend to jump from hobby to hobby, I always find myself coming back to these few ones I mentioned before. I always look to hone my skills and experience new things to broaden my horizons and gain new insights.

Core Values

When faced with the consequences of my own doing, whether it be due to incompetence or a lack of understanding, I will own up to my mistakes and take it as an opportunity to instead learn how to better myself.

I believe honesty is a very important value to have in workplaces and I will do what i can to uphold my own

Open-Mindedness

In discussions with my peers, i tend to adopt a "listen first, speak second" minedset as I enjoy listening to different perspectives brought by peers and co-workers alike.

I find that most times, others will come up with something that has never crossed my mind before, and is usually worth considering.

Teamwork

working in teams with people as opposed to doing solo work is better to me because in teams, you have more opportunities to brainstorm and bounce off of others ideas, as well as cover for each other when necessary.

Teamwork to me, is a essential skill to have as forming bonds with those you work can often lead to many benefits.

Skills

Project Planning

Most if not all projects of mine are always planned out to a tee, every detail included in one way or another.

I find this an essential skill to have as plotting out the vision of projects can help others understand exactly the vision you intend.

Time Management

When it coems to time management, I follow the schedules I make for myself, noting the leneincy I can afford for each time crunch.

I find doing this especially useful as it gives me a way to visualise my small wins, and keep motivation high to keep going, increasing my productivity.

creative thinking

thinking out of the box is something I always enjoy doing, when faced with a problem, I like to theorise and experiment with different ways I can solve the issue.

this usually leads to me taking more time for a task, but finding a more efficient solution than the ones others normally utilise.

Future Goals

I wish to keep honing my skills in coding, especially in game engines like Unreal and Unity as I one day hope to publish my own indie title on Steam. I have been invested into the Mecha genre and aesthetic of games that lean heavily into player customisation and allowing the player freedom in terms of build variety and personalisation.


Something I hope to accomplish is to make or contribute in the making of a title similar to Armored Core, as the old Armored Core games are what got me hooked onto the genre in the first place. However, as of right now, I will continue to focus on improving and refining my skills as a game developer and enjoy the journey.
