My Hobbies

Writing Poetry

I've been writing poetry since i was about 12 years old. I felt it was a great tool for expression as well as creative outlet. I usually write based on what I feel at the moment I'm writing or reflecting on moments that have passed.


I enjoy using poetry as a way for me to express different views from my own and understand how others view the same issues i do in order to try and gain new insights that i can make use of in the future. it has also helped me understand people better and the reasons why some may do certain things.

Playing Games

Games, whether it be on mobile, console or PC, have been a big part of my life since I was young. I was always drawn to the harder spectrum of games as well as the more creative ones.


Sandbox games like Minecraft and Terraria taught me not to limit myself with my imagination and let me run wilds in making my visions come to life.


Meanwhile in the more recent years, Souls games like Dark souls 3, Elden Ring, and Sekiro have taught me that every wall is just another obstacle in your path and a sign to keep pushing through. There is no wall too tough or mountain too big to climb as long as you are determined enough.

Taekwondo

Taekwondo was something i picked up back in Primary school at the age of 9. It was my first CCA and i quite frankly hated it. But as time went on and i got better, i learnt to love it and its intricacies. The small actions, the precise and sharp movements, i fell in love with the sport.


Nowadays, I don't do it as often or as intensely, but the lessons I've learnt from it still stick with me till today. I learnt that even if you dislike something at first, its probably because you aren't good at it, and you should never judge a book by its cover. Only once you get to know something better, and get better at it, should you form an opinion on it.


Till today, i still keep that same mentality with me, which has allowed me to take on multitudes of challenges that i would have otherwise given up on, simply because i didn't like the process at first.

Building Model Kits

Building plastic kits of franchises such as Gundam or Armored core, has been something I've done since childhood. I picked it up from my cousin that used to ring over kits all the time when he visited and I got hooked every since. Assembling these kits take a lot of time, effort, and careful deliberation. every piece has its place and instructions must be followed to a tee if people wish to see the intended final product.


model kit building has taught me how to be patient, follow instructions carefully and keep a level head even when i make mistakes. I've learnt lots just from building the basic kits and even more from the bigger, more advanced, more detailed ones.
